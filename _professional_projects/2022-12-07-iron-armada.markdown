<p class="post-text-center">Iron Armada is a multiplayer, space-faring combat game where players must team up to build a fleet of ships in order to survive the perils of space and defeat the opposing player faction. Build ships of all shapes and sizes, mine resources, and scavenge parts with friends to conquer the universe!</p>

<h3>Technical Contributions</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Mod Management Tool:</strong> Built a Steam Workshop-integrated system allowing players to easily install, update, and manage custom content directly from the game interface</li>
    <li><strong>Shared Radar System:</strong> Engineered a networked radar system enabling players to share tactical information across their fleet, creating new strategic gameplay possibilities</li>
    <li><strong>Modular Sprite System:</strong> Created a flexible sprite architecture supporting custom skin packs, allowing for extensive visual customization</li>
    <li><strong>UI Redesign:</strong> Streamlined the user interface to improve workflow for art team updates and enhance player experience</li>
    <li><strong>Tile System:</strong> Implemented various new tiles including docking bays and missile launchers, with upgrade systems for stats and appearance</li>
    <li><strong>Agile Development:</strong> Managed distributed dev team, ran agile standups, and ensured delivery of iterative builds</li>
    <li><strong>CI/CD Integration:</strong> Maintained GitLab CI pipelines for automated deployment, version control integration, and test validation</li>
</ul>
